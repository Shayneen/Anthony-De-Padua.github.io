Introduction

Greetings and blessings! We are the vibrant and spirited Grade 9 class of St. Anthony de Padua from Immaculate Conception Archdiocesan School – Tetuan. This website is our digital classroom—a space where students, parents, and teachers can stay connected, informed, and inspired.

Here, you'll find our announcements, schedules, assignments, class resources, and a glimpse into the amazing activities we share together. Whether you're checking for updates, downloading study materials, or just browsing through our gallery, we're glad to have you here.

St. Anthony, guide us as we grow in faith, wisdom, and love. Together, let's make this school year meaningful and memorable!
